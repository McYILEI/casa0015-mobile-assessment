// The Problem

Counting in your head doesn't work.

Every existing way of counting pull-ups breaks the workout. Mental counting fails when you're tired. Gym counters are expensive and stuck in one place. Smart watches get forgotten or run out of charge. We needed something different.

Lost focus

Counting in your head pulls attention away from form and breathing.

Lost count

By rep 8, fatigue makes it surprisingly easy to forget the number.

Forgotten wearables

Smart watches require remembering to wear them — and to charge them.

The phone is always there.

Mount it. Start it. Forget it. Let the camera count for you.

// What it does

Everything you need.
Nothing you don't.

Auto rep counting

The front camera and ML Kit pose detection recognise each full pull-up — fully on device.

Phase-based detection

A five-phase state machine — Idle → Hanging → Ascending → AtTop → Descending — prevents miscounts.

Manual adjustment

Tap +1 or −1 to correct the count any time — never lose data to a missed frame.

Set tracking

Record multiple sets per session. Tap "Next Set" to start a new one without losing context.

GPS location tagging

Reverse-geocodes your position to a city or district name — stored with each session.

Stats dashboard

Weekly totals, average per session, cumulative reps, a 7-day bar chart and a 30-session trend line.

Session history

Every workout saved locally — date, duration, total reps, per-set breakdown and location.

Pause & resume

Pause mid-session without losing data. Resume when you're ready for the next set.

// Algorithm

How rep counting actually works.

Four ideas combine to make counting accurate across different bodies, lighting, and movement speeds — without manual calibration.

Filtering

Only frames where ML Kit is confident about the body's position are processed.

nose > 65% · wrists > 60%

Body-size scaling

A "hang distance" is measured at the start, then all thresholds scale to it — works for tall and short users.

baseline = nose ↔ wrist

Anti-bounce

An 800 ms minimum gap between counts removes false positives from small bounces or pauses.

debounce = 800ms

State machine

A rep is only counted after the full five-phase cycle has been completed in correct order.

5 phases · strict order
// Pull-up cycle
Idle → Hanging → Ascending → AtTop → Descending → +1 Rep

// Privacy

Privacy by design.

The phone is the only device that ever sees your data. There is no cloud, no account, no analytics — and no way for anyone but you to look at your sessions.

  • ✓
    Camera frames stay on device Processed in memory, never saved or uploaded.
  • ✓
    No GPS coordinates stored Only the city or district name is kept after reverse-geocoding.
  • ✓
    No accounts, no tracking The app has no sign-in and no analytics SDKs.
  • ✓
    Local SQLite only Session history lives in a single database file on your phone.
